Week 3 (STFI Flood Event): Northeast monsoon dumps 142mm rain. Velachery flooded. NDMA Level 2 alert. Rajan earns only Rs.1,106 (27% of expected Rs.4,050). Trigger fires: Rs.1,106 < 0.60 x Rs.4,050 = Rs.2,430. Payout = 0.75 x (Rs.4,050 - Rs.1,106) = Rs.2,208. Deposited via Razorpay within 4 hours. No manual claim filing needed.
